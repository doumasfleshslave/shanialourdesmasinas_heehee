4th Quarter Events - Credits to: Jade Isabelle S. Imperial

Miting de Avance

Miting de Avance was a lively event where students supported their chosen candidates. The performances and speeches made the program entertaining and exciting. It was interesting to see how students participated in the school election activities. The event also helped us understand the importance of leadership and responsibility.

Streetdance

The Streetdance competition was one of the most fun events this quarter. Students showed their talent and creativity through their dance performances. Practicing the routine required teamwork and dedication from everyone in the group. It was exciting to watch and it made the event very memorable for us.

Battle of the Bands

Battle of the Bands was an exciting event where students were able to showcase their musical talents. Each band performed with energy and passion, which made the competition fun to watch. It was amazing to see how talented many students are in playing instruments and singing. The event created a lively atmosphere and was enjoyed by many students.
